A tribute to Bill Gates

Biography

William Henry Gates III (born October 28, 1955) is an American business magnate, software developer, investor, author, and philanthropist.

He is the co-founder of Microsoft Corporation. During his career at Microsoft, Gates held the positions of chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), president and chief software architect, while also being the largest individual shareholder until May 2014.

He is considered one of the best known entrepreneurs of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s.

Time Line

  • 1955 Born on October 28, 1955 in Seattle, Washington
  • 1967 Goes to Lakeside School. He meets friend Paul Allen.
  • 1973 Enrolls at Harvard University
  • 1975 Drops out of school. Paul & Bill start Microsoft
  • 1980 Microsoft signs important deal with IBM
  • 1988 Paul Allen leaves Microsoft
  • 1994 Bill Marries Melinda French, a Microsoft employee, on the island of Hawaii.
  • 2000 Bill starts the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation
  • 2007 Gets an honorary degree from Harvard
